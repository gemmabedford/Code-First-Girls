Welcome to DUGS

Durham University Geographical Society is one of the oldest and largest student run academic societies within Durham University. With the majority of Geography students being members, along with many students from other degree courses, the society provides an effective link between students from different colleges and departments.

The society aims to provide members with all the information they need about studying geography at Durham and careers after university, whilst providing a wide variety of exclusive social and academic events throughout the year. This year we are aiming to become bigger and better than ever before hosting a large number of guest lectures, freshers, Christmas and summer social events, as well as the infamous Geography Ball.

Feel free to browse the website for more information on DUGS, with details on how to join us available in the 'About Us' section. We hope to meet all of our new members soon in October and very much look forward to welcoming back our current members! 

DUGS love,
​The Exec 2018-2019

Membership

Membership is open to all students of Durham University, enabling you to join DUGS at any given point in the year.  Membership allows you access to speaker events throughout Michaelmas and Epiphany terms, as well as a variety of socials and formal events throughout the year.

Life membership is £21 if you join BEFORE October 10th.
After this date, membership increases to £24.
This year, we are also offering annual membership for £10, for second and third-year students who have not previously joined DUGS
​
Visit the Membership section to learn more about being a DUGS member and for more details regarding pricing and registration.
